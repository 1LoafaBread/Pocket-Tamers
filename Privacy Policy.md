# **Privacy Policy - Pocket Tamers Discord Bot**

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-07

## **1. Introduction**
Welcome to Pocket Tamers! We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe, kid-friendly experience. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

## **2. Information We Collect**

### **A. Automatically Collected Information:**
- **Discord User ID:** Your unique Discord identifier
- **Username & Discriminator:** Your Discord username and tag
- **Server IDs:** The Discord servers where you use the Bot
- **Command Usage:** Which commands you use and when
- **Game Data:** Your creatures, coins, prestige level, and game progress

### **B. Voluntarily Provided Information:**
- **Issue Reports:** Information you provide when using `/report`
- **Privacy Settings:** Your DM preferences set via `/privacy`

### **C. What We DO NOT Collect:**
- **Personal Messages:** We do not read or store your DMs or server messages unless they are direct commands to the Bot
- **Sensitive Data:** We never collect real names, addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, or payment information
- **Voice/Video Data:** We do not access voice channels, video calls, or screen sharing
- **Location Data:** We do not collect your geographical location

## **3. How We Use Your Information**

### **Primary Uses:**
- **Game Functionality:** To save your progress, creatures, and coins
- **Command Processing:** To respond to your commands and requests
- **Leaderboards:** To display weekly rankings (username only)
- **Rate Limiting:** To prevent command spam and abuse
- **Bug Fixes:** To identify and fix technical issues

### **Secondary Uses:**
- **Improvements:** To make the Bot better and add new features
- **Safety:** To enforce our Terms of Service and prevent abuse
- **Analytics:** Anonymous, aggregated data to understand usage patterns

## **4. Data Storage & Security**

### **Storage:**
- **Database:** All data is stored in a secure MongoDB database
- **Location:** Data is stored on secure servers
- **Retention:** Data is kept as long as you use the Bot, or until you request deletion

### **Security Measures:**
- **Encryption:** Database connections are encrypted
- **Access Control:** Only authorized developers can access the database
- **Regular Updates:** Security patches are applied regularly
- **Backups:** Regular backups are performed to prevent data loss

## **5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)**
We take children's privacy seriously:

### **For Users Under 13:**
- The Bot is designed to be COPPA-compliant
- We collect minimal data necessary for gameplay
- Parents can request data deletion for their children
- No personal information is collected from children

### **Parental Rights:**
Parents of children under 13 have the right to:
1. Review their child's personal information
2. Request deletion of their child's information
3. Refuse further collection or use of their child's information
4. Contact us at: contact@1loafofbread.com

## **6. Data Sharing & Disclosure**

### **We Do NOT:**
- Sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing purposes
- Disclose individual user data publicly

### **We May Disclose:**
- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law or legal process
- **Safety:** To protect the rights, property, or safety of users
- **Enforcement:** To enforce our Terms of Service

## **7. Your Rights & Choices**

### **You Can:**
1. **Opt-out of DMs:** Disable Bot DMs with `/privacy allow_dms:false`
2. **Delete Data:** Request account deletion via `/report`
3. **View Your Data:** Request a copy of your data by contacting us
4. **Correct Data:** Report incorrect data via `/report`

### **Data Deletion:**
- Use `/report` to request account deletion
- All your game data will be permanently removed
- This action cannot be undone
- Processing time: 7-14 days

## **8. Third-Party Services**

### **Discord:**
- The Bot operates within Discord's platform
- Discord's Privacy Policy applies to Discord platform usage
- We cannot control Discord's data collection practices

### **Other Services:**
- **MongoDB:** Database hosting service
- **Hosting Provider:** Server hosting service
- We ensure all third-party services have adequate privacy protections

## **9. Data Breach Procedures**
In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:
1. Notify affected users within 72 hours
2. Investigate and contain the breach
3. Take steps to prevent future breaches
4. Comply with applicable data breach notification laws

## **10. International Data Transfers**
If you are located outside Canada, please note that your data may be transferred to and processed in Canada. By using the Bot, you consent to this transfer.

## **11. Changes to This Policy**
We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes by:
- Posting the new policy in our support server
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Encouraging users to review changes
